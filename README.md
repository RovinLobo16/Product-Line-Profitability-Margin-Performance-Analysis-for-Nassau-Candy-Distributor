# Product-Line-Profitability-Margin-Performance-Analysis-for-Nassau-Candy-Distributor

An AI-powered analytics dashboard that transforms raw sales data into actionable insights on profitability, pricing, and operational efficiency.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📊 Executive Dashboard
- Revenue, profit, margin KPIs
- Real-time filtering (division, region, product)

### 📦 Product Intelligence
- Identify high-profit vs low-margin products
- Detect “volume traps” (high sales, low margin)

### 🏢 Division Performance
- Revenue vs profit comparison
- Margin efficiency analysis

### ⚠️ Risk Diagnostics
- Cost vs sales inefficiencies
- Margin risk detection

### 📈 Pareto Analysis (80/20)
- Identify products driving majority of profit

### 🤖 AI Insights
- Automated executive summary
- Risk alerts & recommendations

### 🎯 Pricing Simulator
- What-if pricing scenarios
- Demand elasticity modeling

### 🌍 Geo Analytics
- Factory-level performance
- Profit distribution by location

### 📄 Report Generator
- Auto-generated PDF insights report

---

## 🧠 Business Value

This platform helps answer:

- Which products actually drive profit?
- Are high-sales products hurting margins?
- Where should pricing be optimized?
- Which factories are underperforming?
